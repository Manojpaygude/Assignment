About Us

TravelWell Expeditions is an Australian-owned and operated tour company offering unique remote camping tours with just that little bit extra. With a focus on indigenous culture and connecting to country, our aim is to showcase some of Australia’s most beautiful and isolated locations safely, in comfort and style. With our more popular signature “Starlight Hotel” style of tour, we travel in modern 4WD vehicles, and camp under the stars in a full-size bed, Hotel style. Our tours are for the true adventurous souls who would like to see all the iconic places while avoiding the crowds. The TravelWell experience is designed for all those who crave adventure but enjoy the creature comforts of accommodated journeys. Our expeditions are about experiencing the remote and rugged beauty of the Australian Outback without missing out on luxuries like bathroom facilities. If that sounds like you, then our expeditions are for you.

We cater for all types of small groups, families, couples, and even solo travelers looking to join like-minded explorers. We enjoy creating our private charters, for those looking for something a little extra bit special.

We Go Above And Beyond So Your Once In A Lifetime Experience Is Truly Unique.
